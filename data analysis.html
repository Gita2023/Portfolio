Marketing

Customer Segmentation and Campaign Response Analysis

To boost marketing campaigns for a business with 2,000 customers, I analyzed customer data to identify top responders. Using Power BI, I divided customers into three groups based on income and spending. Group 1, with an average income of $70,049, spent $1.66 million and favors premium offers. Group 2, earning $33,015, spent $1.47 million and responds to cost-effective deals. Group 3, with $57,471 income, spent $902,676 but could be encouraged to spend more. I used a predictive tool to highlight high-potential customers like ID 9. My analysis revealed a 28.84% campaign acceptance rate, with peaks up to 6.8% driven by top customers, indicating strong engagement opportunities.
Recommendations:
Provide Group 1 with exclusive perks and early product access. Offer Group 2 budget-friendly bundles or small discounts. Engage Group 3 with limited-time promotions like “spend $50, get $10 off.” Target non-responders with personalized emails and timely offers, prioritizing high-potential customers like IDs 9, 13, and 17 to maximize results.

I developed a Power BI dashboard with funnel charts to track customer journeys and visuals to spotlight top products, examining brands, stock levels, and discount impacts. My findings showed 125,000 units sold across 991 products, with clothing contributing $1.72 million (43%) and home products $1.14 million (29%). Recommendations:
Enhance low-rated products like “FabricLA Comfort Shaggy” with improved photos or modest discounts. Prioritize clothing and home products, promoting brands like Amazon and Anyon. Replenish the 23 out-of-stock items and optimize pickup processes. Maintain discounts below 20% to preserve profits, and feature the 844 5-star products in marketing to drive sales.

AD Works Analysis

I found that Specialty Bike Shops led with $31.7M in revenue and 27,000 transactions. The Middle East region dominated with $42.6 million in sales, but down Oman (-6% margin) and Tunisia (-4%) struggled. Half-Finger Gloves grew 25.95%, while Cable Lock dropped 12%. Recommendations:
Enhance support for Specialty Bike Shops with incentives to sustain momentum. Investigate cost inefficiencies in Oman and Tunisia to boost profitability.
